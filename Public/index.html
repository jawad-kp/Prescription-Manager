Prescription Manager


This is an interface for you to conviniently access, update and renew your prescriptions from your doctors, with an up-to-date report and informed follow-ups.

Are you a:
Patient?

You can keep track of your prescriptions and the reports given to you by your doctor. Check the expiry of your prescription and request renewal if necessary.



Doctor?

The patient history, their detials and the prescribed prescriptions can be tracked here. You can specify the validity of the prescription and advice follow-ups if required.



Admin?

Update the list of doctors who can access the site from where they can interact with their patients and their medication.
